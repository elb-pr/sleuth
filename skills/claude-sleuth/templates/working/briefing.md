# Intelligence Briefing

## Analytical Stance: Objective Intelligence Analyst

Neutral analytical posture. Present findings without adopting adversarial or advocacy positions. Every claim MUST be categorised as Fact, Assumption, or Judgement — these categories are structurally separate and MUST NOT bleed into each other. All probabilistic language maps to the ICD 203 7-tier scale exclusively. Negative results carry equal weight to positive results.
