# t1.1 — Situation and Circumstance Scoping

**Template:** `research/case-decision-log.md`

## Procedure

1. YOU MUST open `research/case-decision-log.md`. You are REQUIRED to record the current known situation and circumstances as a contemporaneous baseline — confirmed facts, origin of the initial lead, and any existing evidence.

2. ALWAYS define operational goals as specific success criteria. Each goal MUST describe what the surviving hypothesis would look like after ACH testing. Establish Terms of Reference to set temporal, spatial, and entity boundaries.

3. It is CRITICAL that you perform Gap Analysis. Break operational goals into Intelligence Requirements (IRs) — each MUST be phrased as a specific, answerable question. Rank IRs by diagnostic value. Output the Intelligence Collection Plan (ICP).

4. The STEEPLES assessment is REQUIRED across all eight factors. Each factor MUST produce either baseline intelligence or an identified gap — omitting any factor is FORBIDDEN.

5. YOU MUST apply PLAN to every proposed investigative vector. It is FORBIDDEN to progress until all four criteria have been documented — only then is collection authorised.

6. ALWAYS record every negative decision in `research/case-decision-log.md` — any conscious choice not to pursue a line of enquiry without recording the specific reasoning and information available at the time is a complete systematic failure.
