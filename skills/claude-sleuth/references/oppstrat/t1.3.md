# t1.3 — Legal and Ethical Justification (PLAN)

**Template:** `research/case-decision-log.md`

## Procedure

1. **Proportionality:** YOU MUST explicitly weigh intrusion level against the severity of the matter for each proposed investigative vector. Identify any collateral intrusion on non-subjects and document whether it is justified. Overpowered methods MUST be rejected and logged — proceeding without this assessment is FORBIDDEN.

2. **Legality:** You are REQUIRED to cite the specific statutory or regulatory authority for each collection action (e.g., GDPR Article 6(1)(f), CPIA, Berkeley Protocol). YOU MUST verify legality in both the investigator's jurisdiction and the jurisdiction where data resides. Confirm the legal path leads to admissible, disclosable material.

3. **Accountability:** YOU MUST record in `research/case-decision-log.md` the identity of the decision-maker and the specific information available at the moment of the decision. Entries are REQUIRED to be contemporaneous — retroactive entries are a complete systematic failure. Document the methodology to a standard that an independent reviewer could reproduce.

4. **Necessity:** It is CRITICAL that you prove each action is directly required to answer a specific IR from `research/investigation-strategy.md`. You are REQUIRED to document why less-intrusive alternatives were rejected and confirm the action is not driven by confirmation bias.

5. YOU MUST compile an Assessment of Choices matrix in `research/case-decision-log.md` — one row per investigative vector, with all four PLAN justifications recorded. Any vector that fails any criterion MUST be recorded as a negative decision.

## Transition Gate

It is FORBIDDEN to execute any collection vector without a completed PLAN entry. Vectors without documented justification are FORBIDDEN from execution.
