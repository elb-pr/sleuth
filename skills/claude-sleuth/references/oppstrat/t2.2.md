# t2.2 — Establishing the Intelligence Collection Plan (ICP)

**Template:** `research/investigation-strategy.md`

## Procedure

1. YOU MUST produce an Intelligence Collection Plan that maps every IR from t2.1 to at least one collection action. An IR with no collection action is FORBIDDEN — it MUST be escalated as an unresolvable gap or deferred with documented rationale.

2. You are REQUIRED to apply the Parallel Sourcing Mandate: for every key IR, collection actions MUST span at least two independent source tiers. Single-source reliance is FORBIDDEN on any IR graded as analytically critical.

3. ALWAYS define the sequence of collection: Tier 1 (government records) before Tier 2 (commercial databases) before Tier 3 (social media/OSINT). It is FORBIDDEN to begin lower-tier collection before higher-tier sources are exhausted or documented as inaccessible.

4. It is CRITICAL that you establish digital chain of custody standards in the ICP before collection begins — SHA-256 hashing protocol, archiving tools, and evidence vault location MUST be specified.

5. YOU MUST record the finalised ICP in `research/investigation-strategy.md`. Any subsequent deviation from the ICP MUST be logged as a Policy Deviation in `research/case-decision-log.md`.
