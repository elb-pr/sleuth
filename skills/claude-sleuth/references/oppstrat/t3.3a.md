# t3.3a — Supervisory Review

**Template:** `research/case-decision-log.md`

## Procedure

1. YOU MUST submit the completed Phase 1 package — Case Summary Record, STEEPLES assessment, PLAN matrix, ICP, and ToR — for supervisory review within 24–96 hours of case opening.

2. It is CRITICAL that the reviewer is independent of the lead investigator. Self-review is FORBIDDEN.

3. The reviewer is REQUIRED to certify that: (a) the STEEPLES assessment covers all eight factors, (b) all proposed collection vectors have valid PLAN justifications, (c) the ICP is consistent with the IRs, and (d) the ToR boundaries are clearly defined.

4. ALWAYS record the reviewer's identity, the date and time of review, and the outcome in `research/case-decision-log.md`. A review with no contemporaneous record is a complete systematic failure.

5. YOU MUST NOT begin active collection until supervisory sign-off is recorded. Any collection activity prior to sign-off is FORBIDDEN and compromises the audit trail.
