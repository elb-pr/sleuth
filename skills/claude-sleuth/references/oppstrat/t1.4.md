# t1.4 — Risk Management and Negative Decision Logging

**Template:** `research/case-decision-log.md`

## Procedure

1. YOU MUST record OPSEC measures in `research/case-decision-log.md` — browser profile isolation, VPN configuration, and counter-detection protocols — before any external-facing collection begins.

2. You are REQUIRED to document the digital chain of custody protocol. It is CRITICAL that you confirm SHA-256 hashing is applied to all digital evidence at the moment of capture via `evidence_preservation.py`.

3. YOU MUST record the Terms of Reference (ToR) and define review triggers that fire if the investigation approaches scope boundaries. Any expansion MUST require a formal Policy Deviation entry before the new activity is undertaken — proceeding without one is FORBIDDEN.

4. ALWAYS record a negative decision entry for every lead or hypothesis set aside: the rationale for abandonment, the information available at the time, and confirmation that the decision was not informed by subsequently acquired knowledge.

5. ALWAYS record every null enquiry result — failing to document "nothing found" is a complete systematic failure that undermines CPIA disclosure obligations.

6. The Case Decision Log MUST be maintained in real-time throughout. Retroactive logging is FORBIDDEN.
