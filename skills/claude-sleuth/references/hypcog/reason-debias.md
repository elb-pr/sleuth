# Hypothesis Reasoning & Cognitive De-biasing — Phase 5 Overview

Phase 5 transitions the investigation from pattern identification to rigorous hypothesis testing. The governing principle is refutation, not confirmation: the most likely explanation is the one the evidence has the hardest time disproving.

## The Inconsistency Principle

ACH (Analysis of Competing Hypotheses), pioneered by Richards J. Heuer Jr. at CIA, operates on the scientific principle of falsification. YOU MUST NOT seek to prove a favoured theory — you are REQUIRED to calculate which hypothesis accumulates the fewest inconsistencies against the full evidence base.

## Quality Checkpoints

- It is CRITICAL that the matrix evaluates at least three mutually exclusive hypotheses — fewer is a satisficing failure and is FORBIDDEN.
- ALWAYS ensure every evidence item used in the matrix remains linked to its Source Register entry and Admiralty grade from Phase 2.
- YOU MUST derive the narrative conclusion logically from the inconsistency scores. The reasoning for rejecting each alternative hypothesis MUST be documented for inclusion in the final briefing.
- It is FORBIDDEN to combine confidence level and likelihood in the same sentence in the final output.
