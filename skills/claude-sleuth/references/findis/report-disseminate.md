## Phase 6 Quality Checkpoints

- **Confidence/Likelihood Separation:** It is CRITICAL that confidence (evidence quality) and likelihood (event probability) NEVER appear in the same sentence — combining them is FORBIDDEN.
- **Caveat Visibility:** YOU MUST place critical limitations in the main body of the report. Relegating significant caveats solely to footnotes or appendices is FORBIDDEN.
- **Narrative Bias Audit:** You are REQUIRED to explicitly reference the Analysis of Alternatives from Phase 5 to prove contrary data was formally considered.
- **Intelligence Compartmentalisation:** YOU MUST move covert intelligence methods to separate protected schedules (MG6 forms). Accidental disclosure of covert methods in shared products is a FORBIDDEN failure mode.
