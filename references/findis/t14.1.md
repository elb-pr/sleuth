
Task 14.1, **Finalising the Case Summary Record**, completes the administrative record initiated in Phase 1 (Task 3.1a). The Case Summary Record is the high-level administrative "cover sheet" for the entire investigation, now updated to reflect the final status, outcomes, and definitive scope of the completed inquiry.

The detailed procedural breakdown of this task is as follows:

### 1. Final Status Update
The record's status fields are updated to reflect the investigation's conclusion.
*   **Case Status:** Updated from "Active" to the appropriate final status: Completed (all Intelligence Requirements addressed), Closed (investigation terminated before completion, with reasons documented), or Referred (findings passed to another agency or authority for further action).
*   **Completion Date:** The date on which the analytical conclusions were finalised and the briefing was issued.
*   **Duration Record:** Total elapsed time from case opening (recorded in Task 3.1a) to completion.

### 2. Definitive Scope Statement
The initial scope statement drafted in Task 3.1a is reviewed and finalised to reflect what was actually investigated, which may differ from the original plan.
*   **Scope as Executed:** If the investigation's scope expanded or contracted from the original Terms of Reference (via Policy Deviations logged in Task 4.3), the final scope statement documents the actual boundaries of the investigation as conducted.
*   **Addressed IRs:** A checklist of all Intelligence Requirements from the ICP (Task 2.1), indicating which were fully addressed, partially addressed, or not addressed, with reasons.
*   **Out-of-Scope Findings:** If the investigation uncovered significant information falling outside the original ToR (e.g., evidence of unrelated criminal activity), this is documented with a note on how it was handled (referred, flagged, or documented for potential future action).

### 3. Final Team and Authority Record
The record confirms the personnel and authorisation chain for the completed investigation.
*   **Lead Investigator:** Confirmed identity of the individual responsible for the investigation's conclusions.
*   **Team Composition:** Final list of all analysts and investigators who contributed, including any changes from the original team.
*   **Supervisory Sign-Off:** Confirmation that the investigation was reviewed under the supervisory protocols established in Task 3.3a, with the reviewer's identity and the date of final review.

### 4. Cross-Reference to Case File Components
The Case Summary Record serves as the index to the complete case file.
*   **Document Register:** A list of all major components of the case file — the Intelligence Collection Plan, Task Log, Evidence Register, Source Register, Entity Register, Chronological Matrix, Network Analysis outputs, ACH Matrix, and the Analytical Briefing — with their storage locations and access restrictions.
*   **Evidence Vault Reference:** The location of the archived evidence vault (Task 14.3), including confirmation that all digital assets have passed their final hash verification.
*   **Dissemination Log Reference:** A link to the Dissemination Log (Task 14.2) recording who received the investigation's outputs.
